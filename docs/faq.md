## FAQ: Real Talk About AI and Your Future

### Q: Why should I care about AI attribution?

**A: Because your work deserves credit, not corporate profits.** When AI companies scrape your content without permission, they're not just copying your style – they're profiting from your creativity while you struggle to pay bills. This toolkit ensures your human authorship stays human.

### Q: But AI helps me create faster, right?

**A: Sure, until the AI companies decide your work is now "public domain" for their training.** We've seen it happen: artists lose control of their styles, writers get their books scraped, developers watch their code become "AI-generated." Speed today, poverty tomorrow.

### Q: Isn't this just anti-AI fear-mongering?

**A: No, this is reality.** The AI industry is built on taking without asking. Our toolkit doesn't ban AI tools – it just makes sure humans get the credit they deserve. Use AI as your assistant, not your replacement.

### Q: What if my project already uses AI?

**A: Clean it up and start fresh.** Our tools help you remove AI attribution from your commit history, reset your project to human-only contributions, and prevent future violations. It's never too late to take back control.

### Q: How does this protect my copyright?

**A: By enforcing human authorship at the source.** Every commit, every file, every contribution is marked as human-created. No more "Co-authored by GitHub Copilot" lines diluting your ownership. Your work stays yours.

### Q: What about open source projects?

**A: Especially important for open source!** Contributors deserve recognition for their work. AI attribution erases that human connection and weakens the community. Our tools ensure open source stays a human endeavor.

### Q: Will this slow down development?

**A: Actually, it speeds it up.** Clear human authorship means better collaboration, easier code reviews, and stronger project ownership. Plus, you'll sleep better knowing you're not accidentally training the next AI to replace you.

### Q: What if I work for a company that requires AI?

**A: Use it, but don't credit it.** Our policy is simple: AI is a tool, humans are the creators. Keep your commits clean, your authorship clear, and your job secure.

### Q: How do I get started?

**A: Install our toolkit and let it do the work.** It automatically checks every commit, blocks AI attribution, and keeps your project human-first. One command, and you're protected.

### Q: How can I demand that trillionaires pay me for my work and contributions?

**A: Speak up and use our tools to enforce your rights.** The AI industry has made billions from human creativity without fair compensation. Our toolkit helps you maintain control over your work. Contact AI companies, demand payment for data usage, and join the movement for creator compensation. Your contributions deserve fair payment – demand it now.
