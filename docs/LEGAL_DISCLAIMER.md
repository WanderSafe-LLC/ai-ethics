## 2. Intellectual Property & Human Authorship

The core mission of the AI Ethics Enforcement Toolkit is to provide a technical framework to assert and protect human authorship against artificial intelligence generation. However, users must understand the following regarding global IP law:

- **Copyrightability:** Under current frameworks (such as the US Copyright Office doctrines), copyright is generally only strictly granted to works created by a *human author*.
- **Assertion vs. Guarantee:** While this Software automatically flags, removes, or regulates AI attribution to enforce internal company policies, the technical stripping of an AI author's "credit" or the insertion of a human developer's name *does not automatically confer legal copyright status* if the underlying work was generated entirely by a machine without sufficient human creative input.
- **Corporate Policy:** This tool is designed to enforce *your* corporate or personal IP guidelines. It is the user's responsibility to ensure those guidelines comply with applicable local, state, and international IP laws.
