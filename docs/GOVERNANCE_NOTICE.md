---

## Scope of Upstream Governance

The AI Ethics Policy, GitHub↔Notion Synchronization Policy, and related governance documents in this repository apply to:

- `FutureTranz-Inc/ai-dev-tasks` (upstream)
- Contributions submitted to upstream via pull request

---

## Fork Autonomy

Forks of this repository are independent projects. Fork maintainers:

- Are responsible for their own governance decisions
- Are responsible for their own compliance obligations
- Are not bound by upstream governance policies
- May adopt, modify, or disregard these policies as they see fit

---

## Recommendation

We encourage forks to adopt compatible ethics governance. Benefits include:

- Easier upstream contribution
- Consistent user expectations
- Shared best practices

Adoption is voluntary.

---

## Contributing Back

When contributing to upstream:

- Contributions must meet upstream governance requirements
- PRD contributions must include `## AI Ethics & Risk` section
- AI features must complete ethics checklist before merge

These requirements apply only to upstream contribution, not to fork-internal work.

---

## No Warranty

Upstream governance documents are provided as-is. They do not constitute legal advice. Fork maintainers should consult appropriate advisors for their specific compliance needs.

---

## Questions

For governance questions about upstream, open an issue in the upstream repository.

For governance questions about a specific fork, contact that fork's maintainers.
